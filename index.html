Who is Ruchi Pandey?

Passionate, Professional, Ambitious and Communicative, this is what Ruchi Pandey represents. Her passion for fashion is born since her childhood. Clothes are for her a means of expression, the image projected to the outside world. She believes that defining her style is defining her identity, her personality ......
The style: it's the expression of oneself, that's what makes us all unique. Ruchi is currently pursuing her Fashion Technology Course from a Government Polytechnic Jhansi. In school time she has won a lot number of competitions in sketching, painting. The way of thinking of her mind makes Ruchi Stylist, Fashion, and Creative.
